  • Refer below to pre-register for Paper Presentation, Project Presentation and Death Race.

Events:    
1. Paper Presentation    
     This is your platform to express yourself, make your views known, explore your ideas and make the best paper presentation ever. Compete with the sharpest minds in the engineering circuit, lock horns with the best all while standing your ground!
Register Now!
2. Project Presentation    
     With the increase in the outflow of engineers each year, it takes a unique innovative mind to stand out as a leader. Do you have what it takes to invent, reinvent or innovate something worthy of the world's attention? Showcase your ideas and take it a step further!
Register Now!
3. Death Race    
     The flagship event of Mechnovation, the Death Race, is an RC car race, where the riders compete to best one another in terms of speed, acceleration, handling and braking in a unique, unpredictable terrain. Can you make it before others do? The road is rough, the flags are ready, the asphalt is hot, start the engines and ROAR!!
Register Now!
4. General Quiz    
     How good is your general knowledge? Can you blast your way through questions from politics, sports, economics, science and entertainment? Can you be the top quiz whiz of your generation? Compete with similar minds and take the spotlight!
Registration begins on August 22!
5. Cut and Fold    
     You may have tried paper folding in your schooldays. Now, let's follow down the same road, increasing the difficulty, hazard and the chances of errors. Cut and Fold is the perfect contest of speed, efficiency and safety. Do you possess all the three?
Registration begins on August 22!
6. CAD Modelling    
     Do you have a visualising mind? Can you see things take shape in your mind without the need for manufacturing? How fast can you replicate your mind in a CAD software? Test your speed, precision and competency in this computer-based event!
Registration begins on August 22!
7. Engine Assembly    
     The IC Engine, the pinnacle of engineering, the monument of the mechanical engineers, was first invented and assembled in 1859. Can you replicate what those legendary engineers did and race against time to assemble the engine, before your opponents do?
Registration begins on August 22!
8. Treasure Hunt    
     Scattered around the campus are precious objects which will add to your name special points. Be the mechanical detective, decipher the given hints and find those objects before others do. It's time to unlock the Sherlock in you!
Registration begins on August 22!
9. Logo Quiz    
     Are you the industrial freak? How strong is your knowledge of leading ventures? Do you have a special talent for remembering their flagship emblems? It won't be easy. Gear up for the logo quiz! Put forth your memory powers to spot the trickiest of the logos!
Registration begins on August 22!
10. Proto-Car    
     This is an age of innovation and invention. It's not just the design which matters but the speed of invention too. Can you build a prototype of a car which will meet the requirements before your opponents? Gear up for a classical industrial fight where the first wins!
Registration begins on August 22!
11. Adzap    
     As important as invention is to an engineer, without the right marketing and advertisement, any revolution is worthless. Can you turn anything you touch into gold? Are you capable of masterminding an advertisement so good it'll baffle your competition? The world is the audience!
Registration begins on August 22!
12. Auto Quiz    
     Automobiles have been the face of the mechanical branch since time immemorial. A golden field of more than 150 years of age, the automobile industry still blooms with no sign of downfall. Test your acquaintance with the evergreen industry! Sharpen your knowledge and put your mind to the best challenge it can take!
Registration begins on August 22!
13. Mr. Mechanic    
     The jack of all trades is the master of none. But, as an enginner, you are expected to solve all range of problems you face. Can you hurdle past all the twists we throw at you? Excel in design, manufacturing, technical and practical skills and entrepreneurship in this tough fight of wits and muscles equally and take home the crown!
Registration begins on August 22!
14. Technical Debate    
     The skill of communication is in someways dominant over technical knowledge as the latter cannot survive without the former. Sometimes, a good argument can turn the element of truth upside down. Make your speech the finest of the hour and prepare to overturn the tables and prove your point in this vocabulary fight!
Registration begins on August 22!
15. Math Wizard    
     The strongest tool for any engineer, a completely relevant and fundamental element of today's world, Mathematics is this event's arena. Your brain is your weapon. Expel pure magic and be the wizard of mathematics in this nail-biting contest where only the fast come out on top. It's no easy task!
Registration begins on August 22!
16. How Stuff Works    
     Do you have the raw talent of predicting the world before anything happens, using nothing but your knowledge of engineering? Can you explain the universe in the most perfect way? Show your expertise by explaining the imperfections of the physical world with perfection. Be fast or bow out!
Registration begins on August 22!
17. HR Interview    
     Are you the one person that the industry deserves? Take part in this unique event where the vacancy is one, the contestants many. Can you come under intense scrutiny of communication, vocabulary and intellect and still come out with flying colors? The judges are waiting! Steal their attention! It's time to prove how worthy you are!
Registration begins on August 22!
